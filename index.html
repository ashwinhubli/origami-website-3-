Interesting Facts About Cicidas

  • Cicada in Latin means tree cricket.
  • They live on every continent except Antarctica.
  • There are over 2,000 types of cicadas.

    • PANDA

    Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Giant pandas (often referred to as simply “pandas”) are black and white bears.
  • These magnificent mammals are omnivores.
  • Pandas are BIG eaters – every day they fill their tummies for up to 12 hours.

    • CHAMELEON

    Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

    • PIGEON

    Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are known for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

    • BEARS

    Interesting Facts About Bears

  • Bears have a large brain and are one of the more intelligent mammals.
  • Bears take a long sleep in the winter similar to hibernation.
  • Most Bears live alone except when a female has cubs.
